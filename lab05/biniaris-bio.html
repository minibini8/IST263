Christopher Biniaris Biography

Chris grew up in Hong Kong and has always had a very keen interest in computers and how they operate. He always wanted to know how something worked and how the pieces are put together.

In college, he majored in Communications for the first semester and transferred into the I-school very quickly after. He never thought he would be able to have a future in this field.

After sophomore year, Chris landed his first internship with Bank of America, where he spent 10 weeks in Chicago learning the ins and outs of cybercrime. Leading into junior year, he was excited to be able to return.
